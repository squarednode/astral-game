Combat Design
Goal
Combat must feel readable and deliberate before more abilities or enemies are added.
Feedback Layers
A successful hit may combine enemy flash, hit stop, damage number, knockback,
impact VFX, and camera shake. Strength depends on whether the hit is light,
heavy, or a reaction.
Enemy Attacks
Enemy attacks use windup, strike, and recovery phases. Telegraphs must provide
enough time to move or dodge away.
Player Damage
Player damage uses a screen-edge flash, camera bump, and short post-hit
invulnerability to prevent unreadable stacked damage.
Current Exclusions
Version 0.3.0 does not add audio, critical-hit calculations, new enemies,
new abilities, or new AI behaviors beyond attack telegraphs.
